Problem statement: Assignment Organization

There is no time-efficient way to condense and view important assignments and exam due dates.

Team Prototype: Team Prototype: How our app works

Our app includes features such as a calendar icon, a homepage with a graph, and assignment details. The bottom of every page includes a home button, a profile page, and a calendar icon. It also features a list of assignments and a graph tracking progress. The profile includes a photo and several other unique elements of each individual. Settings allow the user to choose display colors and from which position they are viewing the app.

Team Hi-Fi Prototype: Team Hi-Fi Prototype: Our high fidelity app prototype

This video shows our improved/modern app design. It includes features such as a calendar section, a homepage with a graph and assignment details, and a profile. The bottom of every page includes buttons, and the app features a way to see your assignments listed and on the calendar.
